How to Prepare Yourself for Cheer Tryouts...

Assembly time: one month
Awesome rating: 100% awesome

Are you wondering how to prepare yourself for cheer tryouts? It takes hard work but the end results will be worth it! Follow the steps below to learn how to make it onto a cheer team.

  • Things You'll Need

  • Big smile
  • A space to tumble at
  • A space to strecth at
  • Need to practice dances
  • Confidence
  • Healthy eating habits

  1. Research and gather information about the team that you want to tryout for

    This is necessary to know what skills you need to make the team

  2. Start practicing and memorizing the material you have to learn.

    The sooner the better, you want to have as much time as you can to practice.

    Practice is very important

  3. Start streching every night and working out a couple times a week.

    Flexibility is very important when it comes to cheer. Stretching everyday will allow you to perform better and helps the flyer to have better body positions. Working out is also an important aspect, cheerleaders need to have srong muscles.

  4. Tumbling is important when it comes to cheer. Start working and building up your tumbling skills as soon as possible.

    You want to be as prepared as you can.

  5. Have confidence and believe that you can do it.

    You can do anything you put your mind too and hard work pays off!
